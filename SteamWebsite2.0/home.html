Welcome to STEAM – the smart greenhouse

What if we could grow all types of vegetables and fruits anywhere in the world?

Could we cut inefficiencies from the global food chain and offer fresher, healthier, local ingredients?

The solution is STEAM – a computer controlled farming environment. Through the use of code, students and people who never would have dreamed of owning a garden, have now been producing and eating some of their own tomatoes, lettuce and other greens. By controlling factors such as temperature, nutrients, light, and humidity specific “climate recipes” can be perfectly tuned to produce the desired harvests.
